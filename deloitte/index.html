FDE fit

Client problems translated into working GenAI systems.

Deloitte’s Forward Deployed Engineer role calls for someone who can embed with clients, identify valuable GenAI use cases, prototype quickly, and turn successful experiments into maintainable enterprise software. That is the shape of my current work.

I combine hands-on AI engineering with legal-domain judgment and client-facing experience in regulated workflows. My strongest evidence is CrossExamine.AI, a production legal AI SaaS I built end-to-end, plus independent GenAI consulting through Upwork where I scope ambiguous business problems and turn them into working AI workflows.

  • Client engagement: discovery, requirements clarification, stakeholder demos, and tradeoff communication.
  • Solution engineering: LLM workflows, tool use, structured outputs, API integrations, and human review controls.
  • Enterprise readiness: testing, CI/CD, logging, documentation, audit trails, SSO/SAML, and maintainable architecture.
  • Model judgment: quality, latency, cost, explainability, defensibility, and model-risk tradeoffs.
Production build

CrossExamine.AI: defensibility-first legal AI SaaS.

Production AI, built solo.

CrossExamine.AI is a multi-tenant legal AI platform for privilege review, designed around traceability, auditability, and lawyer-readable evidence.

Read the full technical case study →

  • Shipped a full-stack platform with FastAPI, Next.js/React, PostgreSQL, Redis, Docker, Alembic, and GitHub Actions CI/CD.
  • Implemented multi-tenant isolation, WorkOS SSO/SAML, JWT auth, Stripe billing, tier-gated access, and Sentry monitoring.
  • Built a 3-stage privilege detection pipeline using Sparse Autoencoders on Llama-3.1-8B hidden states with GPU extraction via Modal.
  • Integrated Claude Agent SDK for review suggestions with a quality guard that validates quoted text against source documents.
  • Designed human feedback loops, HMAC-signed audit trails, privilege log drafting, Relativity import/export, and defensibility packages.
  • Maintained production discipline through 2,256 pytest/Vitest tests, Ruff, mypy, migration smoke tests, and path-based CI filtering.
Consulting

Independent GenAI consulting through Upwork.

My Upwork work is best understood as client-facing GenAI consulting. Clients usually bring an operational pain point rather than a technical specification. I help clarify the workflow, identify where LLMs can create leverage, prototype the first useful version, integrate with existing tools or data, and iterate based on stakeholder feedback.

The pattern is the same one Deloitte describes for FDE delivery: embed close to the business problem, translate ambiguity into a scoped solution, build quickly, and keep the work anchored to measurable operational value.
